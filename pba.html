Pro Bono Achievements

Every other year, Kirkland would publish a book to celebrate the pro bono efforts of its attorneys. I was given the challenge of adapting the piece into a digital medium. Our team quickly realized that the digital PDF initially considered presented unsatisfying limitations. I proposed a microsite as a solution, which gave us the freedom to expand beyond text stories and include multimedia elements not previously possible in a printed work, such as accompanying social media campaigns. Subsequent editions have continued in a web-based format.
